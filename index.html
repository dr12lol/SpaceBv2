About us

Established in 2032, SpaceB is a pioneering space agency dedicated to the advancement of human civilisation beyond Earth. Our primary mission involves the development and implementation of terraforming technologies to create habitable environments on distant planets and moons. At SpaceB, we are committed to providing more space for human civilisation, fostering opportunities for long-term stays and even vacation opportunities in space. Through innovation and dedication, we aim to expand the horizons of human presence in the cosmos, ensuring a sustainable and thriving future for generations to come.
